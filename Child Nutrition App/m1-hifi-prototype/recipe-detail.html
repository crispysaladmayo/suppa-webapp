Chicken & carrot rice bowl

Per serving (estimate) · ~320 kcal · 18g protein · 30g carbs · 12g fat

Protein-forward

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice, 2 cups
  • Chicken thigh, diced
  • Carrot, sliced
  • Garlic, oil, salt

Steps

  1. Sauté chicken until cooked through.
  2. Add carrot and cook until tender.
  3. Fold in rice; season to taste.
